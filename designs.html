The Johhny Cash Project involves contributing hand-drawn frames from participants in order to form a new music video for American singer Johnny Cash's song '"Ain't No Grave". Submitted drawings deemed fit to be accepted into the video are made into part of the final sequence, leaving a unique portrait of Johnny Cash. Here are a few frames of mine that got accepted! Click on any of them to view a timelapse of their drawing process.
